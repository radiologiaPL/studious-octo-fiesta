Privacy Policy

Radiologia Polska Publishing Application

Effective date: July 7, 2026

This privacy policy describes the data practices of the software application ("the App") used to publish and manage content on the Facebook Page Radiologia Polska. The App is operated by the administrators of the Radiologia Polska Facebook Page ("we," "us").

What the App Does

The App is an internal publishing tool. Its sole function is to create, schedule, and manage posts on our own Facebook Page using the Meta Graph API. It is used exclusively by our administrators and is not offered to, installed by, or made available to the general public.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data from Facebook users. It does not read user profiles, comments, messages, friend lists, or any other user-generated content. It does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on any third party.

The only data the App handles are (a) access tokens issued by Meta to authorize publishing to our own Page, and (b) the content of the posts we ourselves author.

How Information Is Used

Access tokens are used solely to authenticate publishing requests to our Page and are stored securely by our administrators. Post content is transmitted to Meta for publication and is subject to Meta's own terms and data policies once published.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share any data with third parties. No user data is transferred to the App from Meta beyond the authorization tokens described above.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because the App does not collect personal data from users, there is no user data to retain or delete. If you believe the App holds any data relating to you and wish to request its deletion, contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly. You may also revoke the App's access at any time through your Facebook settings under Settings & Privacy → Apps and Websites.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued operation of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
